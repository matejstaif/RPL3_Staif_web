Head Coach

Petro Nakonechnyi

01

Petro Nakonechnyi is a Ukrainian kickboxer in welterweight, originally from Odessa from the Captain Odessa club. He is the champion of K-1 MAX Ukraine 2006 and K-1 Europe MAX 2008 in Poland.

02

After making a name for himself by winning many national and world titles in muay thai, Nakonechnyi entered K-1 East Europe MAX 2006 in Lithuania, defeating Belarusian fighter Vasily Shish. On November 24, 2006, he entered the eight-member Grand Prix at K-1 MAX Ukraine 2006 and won the tournament when he defeated Dmitry Konstantinov, Alexander Medvedev and Artem Sheglov.

03

In March of the following year he was invited toparticipation in the Grand Prix at K-1 East EuropeMAX 2007 in Ukraine, but was eliminated Yuri Boreik in the quarterfinals. In April. However, the K-1 Europe MAX 2008. He returned Poland as he advanced. Andrej Buda and Yuri Harbačov and in the final of the tournament defeated Michal Głogowski.
